A Review of Smart Condition Monitoring System for Gearbox

Project information

  • Category: Reseach Project
  • Guide: Dr. J. Ramkumar & Dr Amandeep Singh
  • Project Duration: May 2021 - December 2021
  • Publication Link: A Review of Smart Condition Monitoring System for Gearbox

  • This paper presents a literature review of the different IoT-based techniques used to acquire health status data from the gearbox. To understand the process, the manuscript presents the basic information on the gear types and defects in them in the first section.
  • The study also highlights the Opportunities and Challenges in monitoring gearbox health
  • This studies portray that meaningful Data Acquisition using sensors from the Gearbox is an open challenge hitherto.The authors found that the estimation of the remaining useful time of Gearbox even more challenging, and the condition indicators already developed could be able to extract the features needed for future detection methods.
