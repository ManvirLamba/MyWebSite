Assembly and Design of Drive Mechanism and Support Frame for Force Pump driven by HONDA GX160 engine

Project information

  • Category: Academic Project
  • Project Duration: May 2021 - June 2021
  • Project Report: Link

  • Assembly of HONDA GX160 engine with the crankshaft and the feed pump was carried out.
  • Designed and modelled a drive mechanism consisting of a belt and pully, gear reduction and scotch and yoke mechanism to transfer motion from the engine to the feed pump.
  • Designed a support structure to house the feed pump, drive mechanism and engine.
  • Created production drawings of all parts and assemblies.
