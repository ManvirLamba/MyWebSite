About

I am Manvir Singh Lamba, a junior undergraduate at Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology (Deemed University), Patiala, India, pursuing a Bachelor of Engineering in Mechatronics; expected to graduate in 2023.

Robotics Enthusiast and an upcoming researcher versed in independent and team-based projects to enhance robotic systems' research, design, development, and testing. Skilled technologist with project-based experience in modeling, designing, and analysis using PTC Creo, Solidwork, and Ansys. Strong programming experience in developing robotics and IoT-based applications using ROS, C++, Python, Arduino, Rasberry PI, and MATLAB. I have experience in constraint programming techniques in solving mobile robots' SLAM-based problems.

I am an undergrad researcher in the System and Control lab, TIET, working on soft robotics under the guidance of Dr. Tarun Kumar Bera. Currently, a Research Intern at the Mechatronics Lab, Indian Institute Of Technology, Patna, working on bio-inspired robotics under the mentorship of Dr. Atul Thakur.
